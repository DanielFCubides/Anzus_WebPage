Our company

We have a short history with many plans for the future.

Origin

Anzus Development was founded in 2014 as an initiative to develop projects by a student group of systems engineering at the National University of Colombia, who are in constant contact with new technologies, looking for innovation in the area since the computing market is constantly changing. This company aims to contribute to the creation of services, and the promotion of employment in the ​information technologies's field which are becoming more necessary every day to the country's development.

Mission

Anzus Development aims to design, develop and maintain software products that provide solutions to problems based on innovative technological own initiatives. working in different areas like mobile applications, web applications and game development.

Vision

Anzus Development plans to position itself in the Colombian market software with new ideas and high quality products that allow it to be a technological solution for Colombian companies on December 2019.
